## 💬 Tone of Voice

### Brand Voice Attributes
| Attribute | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Tone** | [Professional / Friendly / Casual / Formal] | "We help you..." vs "Let's help you..." |
| **Personality** | [Adjectives: innovative, trustworthy, playful] | Use active voice, conversational |
| **Language Level** | [Simple / Technical / Mixed] | Avoid jargon unless B2B tech |

### Do's & Don'ts
| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|------|---------|
| Use active voice | Use passive voice |
| Keep sentences short (< 20 words) | Write long, complex sentences |
| Address reader directly ("you") | Use third person ("users") |
| Include specific numbers/data | Use vague terms ("many", "some") |
| Use power words (proven, guaranteed) | Use generic phrases ("world-class") |

## 📊 Content Types & Specifications

### Homepage
- **Headline**: 6-10 words
- **Subheadline**: 15-20 words
- **Hero section**: 50-100 words
- **Features section**: 3-5 features, 30-50 words each
- **Social proof**: 2-3 testimonials
- **CTA**: Above fold + end of page

### Product Page
- **Product title**: 5-8 words
- **Short description**: 50-75 words
- **Long description**: 150-300 words
- **Features**: 5-7 bullet points
- **Specifications**: Table format
- **Reviews**: Display 3-5 top reviews

### Landing Page
- **Headline**: 6-10 words, benefit-focused
- **Subheadline**: 15-20 words
- **Body**: 200-400 words
- **Features**: 3-5 sections, 50-100 words each
- **Social proof**: Stats, testimonials, logos
- **CTA**: Multiple placements (hero, mid-page, footer)

### Blog Post
- **Title**: 60-70 characters (SEO optimized)
- **Meta description**: 150-160 characters
- **Introduction**: 100-150 words
- **Body**: 800-1500 words
- **Subheadings**: Every 200-300 words
- **Conclusion**: 100-150 words, include CTA

### Email Campaign
- **Subject line**: 40-50 characters
- **Preview text**: 80-100 characters
- **Body**: 150-300 words
- **CTA**: 1 primary CTA, clear and prominent
- **P.S.**: Optional, add urgency or bonus

### Social Media Ad
- **Headline**: 5-7 words
- **Body**: 125 characters (Twitter) / 125-150 words (Facebook)
- **CTA**: 2-3 words
- **Image**: 1200x628px with text overlay

## 🚨 Common Copywriting Mistakes

| ❌ Mistake | ✅ Fix |
|-----------|--------|
| Feature-focused ("We have X") | Benefit-focused ("You get X") |
| Passive voice ("It can be done") | Active voice ("You can do it") |
| Jargon/buzzwords ("synergy") | Plain language ("work together") |
| Long paragraphs (> 5 sentences) | Short paragraphs (3-4 sentences) |
| Weak CTA ("Click here") | Strong CTA ("Get Your Free Trial") |
| No social proof | Include testimonials/stats |
| Generic copy ("best quality") | Specific copy ("4.8/5 stars, 1000+ reviews") |
