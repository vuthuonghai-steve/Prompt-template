## ✍️ Writing Style

### Do's
- ✅ Use active voice
- ✅ Keep sentences short (< 20 words)
- ✅ Use bullet points for lists
- ✅ Include clear CTAs

### Don'ts
- ❌ Avoid jargon (unless technical audience)
- ❌ No passive voice
- ❌ No generic phrases ("world-class", "cutting-edge")
