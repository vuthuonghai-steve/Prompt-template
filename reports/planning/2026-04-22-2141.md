### 1. Information Architecture Prompt for E-commerce Sites
- **Quality Score**: 8/10
- **Source**: https://www.promptingguide.ai/applications/information-architecture
- **Strengths**:
  - Structured approach to organizing product categories and navigation
  - Includes user persona consideration
  - Scalable for large product catalogs
- **Weaknesses**:
  - Requires manual refinement for niche markets
  - Limited mobile-first considerations
- **Use Cases**:

  - Multi-level navigation design
  - Product taxonomy creation
